E - mnemonic: "Equip" - command for dealing with inventory
<e>   - bring up inventory screen
<E>   - bring up equipped items screen

R - mnemonic: "Rest"
<R>   - rest for x turns

A - mnemonic: "Attack"
<a>   - attack with weapon in x cardinal direction
<A>   - designate target

C - mnemonic: "Close"
<c>   - signal OPEN  (ironic, but most of the time you'll use c to close doors)

D - mnemonic: "Drop"
<d>   - pick up item (also ironic, but it's the best mnemonic I could think up)
<D>   - drop item from inventory  (possibly put this as a <d> command in the inventory screen)
