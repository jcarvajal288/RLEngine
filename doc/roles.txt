Soldier - starts with firearms training, pistol, longarm, (flak jacket?)

Gangster - high hand-to-hand combat skills, proficiency with bats, knives.  Small chance for Thompson. Underworld connections.

Detective - Firearms training, pistol, fedora, flashlight.  Decent money.

Scientist - high research skills, no weapons training, decent money, perhaps scientific tools?  Good at learning magic.

Occultist - only class that starts with magic. Low money, no weapons training.  High chance to go mad.

Doctor - Good at healing.  High money.  Little weapons training.

Eccentric - Random skills, random inventory.  Low chance to have a magic item or two.
